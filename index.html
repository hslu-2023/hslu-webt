About

Welcome to TravelTales: your ultimate platform for documenting and sharing your travel experiences! Our user-friendly web application allows you to effortlessly record your adventures and visualize your journeys on an interactive world map.

To start capturing your leisure travels, simply fill out the form below with essential details such as Title, Reason, Country, City, Latitude, Longitude, and a brief description. Note that only leisure travels will be displayed! It's important to provide all the information for your records to be accurately saved. Once you've completed the form, just hit the " Add Trip" button, and voila!

Scrolling down, you'll find a card displaying the details of your newly added trip. Further down the page, explore the dynamic world map, where each dot represents a place you've visited.
Get ready to embark on a virtual journey and add as many trips as you'd like!

Happy exploring and safe travels! :)
